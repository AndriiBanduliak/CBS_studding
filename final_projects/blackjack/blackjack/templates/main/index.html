Game Rules

  • Cards 2-10 are worth their face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10
  • Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever is more advantageous
  • The goal is to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over
  • Blackjack (an Ace and a 10-value card) pays 3:2

Player Actions

  • Hit: Take another card
  • Stand: Take no more cards
  • Double Down: Double your bet and take one more card
  • Split: Split a pair into two separate hands

Dealer Rules

  • The dealer must hit until their hand totals 17 or higher
  • The dealer's first card is face up, the second is face down
  • If the dealer busts, all remaining players win
